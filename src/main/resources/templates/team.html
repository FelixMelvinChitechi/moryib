EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY Moryibrah Logistics Ltd was established in 2020 and has a national structure of industries that does businesses in over twenty counties nationally and has the capacity to employ over 150 employees. Moryibrah business Logistics is a business planning framework that is concerned with materials procurement, materials management and overall inventory control. Our main activity is to take goods from source to destination location effectively and help reduce the carrying and storing costs of inventory and increase profit flow in the organization.

IMPORTANCE OF MORYIBRAH LOGISTICS LTD


Our services include the unbound as well as the outbound movement of inventory and supplies. We estimate the demand of the organization, the requirements of the organization for the project at hand. We decide the sources of procurement of materials and the method of procurement of materials. Exercise control over the physical movement of inventory between departments and also concerned with the process of storing the inventory at various stages so as to achieve an optimum level of demand and supply and reduce costs. We combine physical distribution and materials management apart from dealing with the process of procurement.
